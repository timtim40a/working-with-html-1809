INT CAFE - Evening

[A quiet, dimly lit café. A young woman, LISA (mid-20s), sits at a corner table, stirring her coffee absentmindedly. The door opens with a soft chime, and JAMES (late 20s) walks in, scanning the room.]

CAMERA: Medium shot – James enters, pausing at the door.

JAMES
(softly, hesitant)
Lisa?

CAMERA: Close-up – Lisa looks up, surprised.

LISA
(smirking)
You actually showed up.

CAMERA: Over-the-shoulder – James approaches, hesitant. He pulls out the chair opposite her and sits down.

JAMES
(nods)
Yeah... I thought we should talk.

CAMERA: Two-shot – Both in frame, tension lingering. Lisa sighs, tapping her fingers against her mug.

LISA
You realise it’s been two years?

JAMES
I know.

(beat)

JAMES
I just... I wasn't ready before.

CAMERA: Close-up on Lisa – She studies him, trying to read his intentions.

LISA
And now you are?

[JAMES hesitates, then nods.]

FADE TO BLACK.
